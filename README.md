# Mental Health Analysis Dashboard – Power BI

A Power BI dashboard analyzing mental health trends among university students. Covers key factors including CGPA, academic pressure, financial concerns, sleep patterns, social relationships, and coping activities. Built with interactive filters for gender, academic year, and university across 2 report pages.

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Academic & Mental Health
- CGPA vs Financial Concerns & Academic Pressure
- Sports & Coping Activities analysis
- Social Relationships vs Mental Health
- Filters: Gender, Academic Year, University

### Page 2 – Well-Being & Residential Analysis
- Residential Status vs Satisfaction & Sleep
- Mental Health Breakdown by Academic Pressure
- CGPA & Mental Health by University and Degree Level
- Well-Being Trends by Academic Year

## Key Metrics
- Average CGPA: 3.38
- Average Sleep Score: 566.67%
- Average Mental Health Score: 327.75%
